How to contribute to BISmark

As with many projects, BISmark and its upstream projects are built through the efforts of volunteers, and there is always more work to be done than hours in the day. If you are interested in contributing towards the goals of BISmark or its upstream projects, please contact us at bismark-core@projectbismark.net.

Some ideas for how to get involved include:

  • Testing beta releases on your router
  • Squashing bugs, writing documentation, or porting packages for BISmark and its upstream project OpenWrt
